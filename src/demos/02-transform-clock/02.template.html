Demo 2: Transform Clock

The current time is:

In this demo, points are rotated around the z-axis using the engine's transform functions.

The hour, minute, and second indicators are positioned by converting the parts of the current time to radians and using those values to calculate a rotation on each tick.

The hour and minute hands are heavily quantized — this is because they are calculated by dividing hours by 12 and minutes by 60 before converting to rotation. We could fix this by converting them to seconds and dividing by (12×60) and (60×60) instead.
